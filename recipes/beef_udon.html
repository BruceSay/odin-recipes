Beef Udon

Description

Beef Udon. Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 300g thinly sliced beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves chopped
  • 800g udon noodles
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 spring onion
  • 2 tsp cornstarch

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water
  2. Heat oil in a wok. Stir fry onions for a few minutes. Add the beef and sear. Add the garlic and stir fry. Turn off the heat
  3. Boil the noodles, then add to the wok. Add the sauces and seasoning
